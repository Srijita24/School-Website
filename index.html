CAMPUS LIFE

Library Hall

This Is The Place Where Students Can Mark A Place In There Lives

We at Griffins International School are firm believers of the proverb ‘Readers are the Leaders’. We have 1650 sq. ft. of space specially dedicated for the school library. Our library is the storehouse of knowledge and books. It offers more than 4000 books along with daily news, weekly periodicals and monthly/ annual subscriptions for children and teachers. In addition, e-books and audio-visual aids are also available for the school community. We have interlinked our academics along with books in such a way that it focuses on developing the reading habits in children through celebrating theme based weeks, such as Science Week, Story week, Poetry Week, etc. These activities not only help children to develop interest in reading and enhancing reading skills but they also open the doors of knowledge and wisdom.

Music Studio

Students Perform From There Soul Here.

The music studio is well equipped to teach students instrumentals and vocal skills. The vocal teacher looks after sharpening the singing abilities of our students.The instrumental teacher works on developing the ability of students to play various instruments like Guitar, Piano, Cajon, keyboard, drums etc. The school band is an integral and vital part of the school. The music department organizes shows for students to inspire them to perform better.

Swimming Pool

Students Stay Finger Crossed For Attending Swimming Class To Enjoy Themselves.

The school has a state of art 8 lane swimming pool for the senior kids and splash pool for the small kids. Swimming helps develop stamina and strength in the students. The students are taught this important life skill of swimming and also about saving someone’s life during exigencies in water.

WHAT'S NEW

  • The School Has Created Four Houses - Kite, Hawk, Falcon And Phoenix

    Phoenix depicts VICTORY. The values given here are that students should have a competitive spirit and should try and win all the tasks given to them.Kite depicts BRAVERY. It inculcates courage to face any situation upfront with utmost bravery.Falcon depicts HONESTY. It imbibes a sense of utmost honesty among the students. They are preached to be the harbinger of peace in the society and Hawk depicts WISDOM. It teaches students to be wise and kind to each and every being on earth.

  • The School Currently Focuses On Making Children Aware About The Earth

    The burning of fossil fuels, in both energy plants and vehicles, releases massive amounts of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere, causing climate change.Griffins believes that, children are the one who can save the Earth from this critical moment by saving it and making a beatiful place for living. So, the school makes the students engage themselves in the "Earth Ambassador Project" so that they become aware about the day-to-day carbon emissions caused to the environment and take the aim to spread this awareness among all.
